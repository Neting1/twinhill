Frequently Asked Questions

What are your business hours?

We are open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and on Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer delivery services for both retail and wholesale customers. Delivery charges may apply depending on the location and order size.

How can I become a wholesale partner?

To become a wholesale partner, please contact our sales team through the contact form or visit our office. We'll guide you through the registration process and discuss pricing options.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, mobile money transfers, and bank transfers. For established wholesale partners, we also offer credit facilities with approved terms.
